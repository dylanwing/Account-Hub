Quick Response Objections
"Databricks is the market leader"
True, but they're optimized for data processing, not ML. Ray was built for distributed ML. Our customers see 3x faster training and significantly lower costs because we're ML-first.
"We're already on AWS with SageMaker"
SageMaker works, but you're locked in to AWS and pay AWS tax. Anyscale works across all clouds. Try deploying your SageMaker model to Azure - it's painful. With Anyscale, you retain portability.
"Modal is simpler and cheaper"
Modal is great for simple inference. But when you need to train models at scale or serve production traffic, Modal hits hard limits. We're designed for the entire ML lifecycle.
"Together AI has cheaper inference"
Together AI does inference cheap, but has zero training capabilities. If you're training custom models, you're paying 2x for separate platforms. Anyscale's unified platform saves money across the entire workflow.
"Our team is already trained on Spark/Databricks"
Fair point, but Ray is actually easier for ML than Spark. In 2-3 weeks, your team will be productive on Ray. The upside is enormous - faster model development, lower costs, and flexibility your current setup doesn't have.
"We need AutoML like Vertex AI offers"
AutoML is good for simple use cases, but gives you black-box models. Production ML teams want control. Ray + Anyscale gives you both: tools for quick experimentation AND full control when you need custom models and algorithms.
