🏢 Company Overview

LlamaIndex is the industry standard Python-native framework for building enterprise RAG agents, with 3M+ monthly downloads and growing fast. LlamaCloud SaaS platform manages indexing, retrieval, and evaluation at scale. As enterprises scale RAG to thousands of documents with multi-step reasoning, LlamaIndex needs distributed indexing and retrieval capabilities that Ray provides natively.

❌ Pain Points

  • Distributed indexing of massive document corpuses (100k+ docs) requires parallel processing and embedding generation
  • Enterprise RAG scaling: coordinating multiple retrievers, re-rankers, and evaluators across clusters
  • Serving embeddings and reranking models on GPUs with stateful retrieval logic

⚔️ Competitors

Modal, AWS Bedrock, LangChain, Pinecone

✅ Why Anyscale

  • Ray Data parallelizes document chunking and embedding generation—reduce indexing time 10x
  • Ray Serve handles multi-GPU embedding models and rerankers with automatic scaling
  • Natural integration: LlamaIndex indexing + Ray serving = complete enterprise RAG stack
  • LlamaCloud powered by Ray (managed RAG-as-a-service)

Ray Fit: Ray Data for distributed document chunking, embedding, and index building. Ray Serve for multi-GPU serving of embedding models and rerankers. Ray AIR for end-to-end RAG pipeline scaling.
